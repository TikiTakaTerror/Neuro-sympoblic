# Thesis Notes

## Working Thesis Direction

The thesis focuses on fair benchmarking of neuro-symbolic systems rather than proposing a new model family.

## Key Framing

- The benchmark should separate performance from explanation
- Final accuracy alone is not enough
- The symbolic layer should be assessed through internal contribution measures
- Fairness depends on controlling supervision, capacity, and training budget as much as possible

## Questions To Keep Visible

- Does the symbolic layer improve final task performance?
- Does the symbolic layer improve concept quality?
- Does the symbolic layer improve semantic consistency?
- Does the symbolic layer reduce shortcut-like behavior?
- Do conclusions change under stricter controls?
